Self-Diagnose Your System

Real talk about repairs: Every system is unique. Age, model, accessibility, and what actually failed all affect repair complexity. This tool gives you likely scenarios, but your actual situation needs hands-on diagnosis. We'll always explain what we find and your options before any work begins.

Click a Symptom:

Example Guidance:
  • System won't turn on: Check your thermostat settings, batteries, and breaker. Still nothing? Likely causes: blown fuse, failed capacitor, or control board issue. Text us for help.
  • Ice forming: Dirty filter, low refrigerant, or restricted airflow. Shut off your system and let it thaw before running again. Call for service if the issue repeats.
