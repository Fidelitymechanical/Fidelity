What areas of Houston do you serve?

Why is my Houston AC running but not cooling?

Common causes include dirty filters, low refrigerant, frozen coils, or thermostat issues. Houston's humidity can make these problems worse. We'll diagnose the exact cause and fix it quickly.

How often should I service my AC in Houston?

Houston's hot, humid climate means your AC works harder. We recommend service twice yearly - spring and fall. Regular maintenance prevents breakdowns during Houston's brutal summers.
